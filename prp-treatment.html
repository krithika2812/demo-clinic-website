PRP Hair Treatment in Chennai

PRP hair treatment helps support natural hair growth and reduce hair fall using platelet-rich plasma therapy.

PRP Treatment Procedure

The treatment involves collecting the patient's blood, separating platelet-rich plasma, and injecting it into the scalp.

Benefits of PRP Hair Treatment

  • Helps reduce hair fall
  • Supports natural hair growth
  • Non-surgical treatment
  • Minimal downtime

Related Treatments

PRP treatment can also support patients after hair transplant treatment.
